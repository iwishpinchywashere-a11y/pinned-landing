Common questions.

Short answers. If you have something else, email hello@pinned.fit.

Is Pinned free?

Yes. Core features — tracking, calculator, library, vial inventory — are completely free, with no subscription. AI Coach has a generous free tier with optional upgrade for unlimited usage.

Is it on iOS and Android?

Both. Native App Store and Play Store builds are in private beta — join the waitlist to get early access.

Is my data private?

Yes. Your protocols, dose history, and vials are stored in your account — encrypted in transit and at rest. We never sell or share your data with third parties. You can export everything as JSON at any time.

How accurate is the calculator?

The reconstitution math is exact: dose ÷ concentration, with concentration derived from peptide mass and BAC water volume. We then snap to the nearest U-100 unit mark for the syringe size you've selected. Forward and reverse modes use the same formula in different directions — there's no fudge factor.

Does the AI Coach give medical advice?

No. Pinned Coach is an educational tool only. It surfaces research-backed information about your stack, but it can't replace a qualified healthcare provider. Always consult a professional before starting, modifying, or stopping any peptide.

Can I import my existing protocol?

Yes. Pick from our pre-built stacks, customize one, or build from scratch in the Calculator. Setup takes under 2 minutes and the Coach can help you migrate from a spreadsheet if you have one.
