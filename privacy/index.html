Privacy
Policy.

Last updated: May 2026

The short version

Pinned is a tracking tool you control. We collect the bare minimum to make the app work and keep your account secure. Your stack, doses, and vial data live on your device first. Anything synced to our servers is encrypted in transit and at rest.

We don't sell your data. We don't run ads. We don't share what you log with anyone outside of Pinned.

What we collect

Account info: your email address and password hash (handled by Supabase Auth — we never see your raw password).

Profile info: anything you optionally type into Profile — name, age, sex, height, weight, goals. All optional. Stored encrypted on our servers.

Tracking data: peptides, doses, vials, schedules, and dose history you save. Stored locally first and synced to our servers when you're logged in.

Coach conversations: the text of messages you send to Coach and the responses, so the chat persists across sessions. Sent to Anthropic's API for processing.

Payment info: handled by Stripe (web) or Apple/Google (mobile). We see your subscription status; we never see your card details.

Crash + usage logs: basic diagnostics (app version, device type, error stack traces) to keep the app stable. No personal data attached.

How we use it

To run the app for you: render your Today screen, surface your inventory, answer Coach questions about your stack.

To improve the app: bug reports, version-to-version stability tracking, anonymous feature usage so we know what to build next.

To process payments: forwarding subscription events to Stripe or the app store.

We do not use your data to train models. Your Coach conversations are not used by us or Anthropic for training under our API contract.

Who we share it with

Supabase (our auth and database provider) — stores your encrypted account and tracking data.

Anthropic (the AI behind Coach) — receives the text of your Coach messages so it can generate a response. Anthropic does not retain or train on this data per our API agreement.

Stripe / Apple / Google — handle payments. They see what they need to charge you; we see only the subscription status.

Nobody else. Not advertisers, not data brokers, not analytics resellers.

Your rights

Export: hit Export in your Profile to download a JSON file of everything you've ever logged.

Delete: hit Delete account in Profile — your account, profile, doses, vials, schedules, and Coach history are removed from our servers within 7 days.

Correct: edit any field at any time. Nothing is locked.

If you're in the EU/UK/California and want to exercise a specific GDPR / UK GDPR / CCPA right (access, portability, restriction, etc.), email hello@pinned.fit and we'll respond within 30 days.

Security

TLS in transit. AES-256 at rest. Auth tokens scoped per session and rotated. Service worker caches the app shell locally so most of Pinned works without network access.

No system is perfectly secure. If we ever experience a breach affecting your data, we'll notify you by email within 72 hours of confirming it.

Kids

Pinned is not intended for anyone under 18. We don't knowingly collect data from minors. If you believe a minor has used the app, email hello@pinned.fit and we'll remove the account.

Changes

We'll update this policy if our data practices change. Material updates get a notice in the app and an email to your account address.

Contact

Questions about privacy: hello@pinned.fit. We respond to every email.
