Calculator

The math,
done right.

Tell it your vial size and your dose. It tells you exactly how much water to add and which line to draw to on your syringe. No spreadsheets, no guessing, no mixing up mL with units.

  • Calculate water — picks a clean syringe tick (10, 15, 20 u) for you
  • Calculate units — for vials you've already mixed
  • Stack & Blend mode — multi-peptide vial math, automated
  • Won't-fit warning if your dose overflows your selected syringe

Vial Scanner

Scan a vial.
Get an A+ to F.

Point your camera at any vial label. Pinned reads the vendor and cross-references it against 270+ independently rated suppliers — sourced from Finnrick, Janoshik, and PeptideScores public lab data.

  • Instant letter grade based on real lab purity data
  • Auto-fills your inventory — vendor, lot, stability countdown
  • Catches sketchy vendors before you reconstitute, not after
  • Updated daily as new lab tests publish
