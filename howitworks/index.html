How it works

Five screens.
One job each.

No menus to memorize. Every page in Pinned does one specific thing, really well.

01
Pinned (home)

Your daily home.

Every peptide you're running, all on one screen. Each card shows what to take, when it's due, and how to draw it. One tap to log a dose; the card greys out and shows when the next dose is coming.

  • Big countdown to your next dose
  • One-tap logging — no menus, no forms
  • Streak counter so you don't skip days
  • Site-rotation warnings when you're overusing one spot
  • Quick scan-vial button for new bottles
02
Calculator

The math, done right.

Tell it your vial size and your dose. It tells you exactly how much water to add and which line to draw to on the syringe. No spreadsheets, no guesswork, no mixing up mL with units.

  • Calculate water — picks a clean syringe tick (10, 15, 20 u) and gives you the exact water amount
  • Calculate units — for vials you've already mixed
  • "Suggest easiest mix" snaps the water amount to a round draw when possible
  • Stack & blend mode — multi-peptide vial math, automated
  • Warns you when a dose won't fit your selected syringe
03
Tracker

Every dose. Every vial.

Three tabs in one screen: History, Body, and Inventory. Adherence, streaks, dose-by-dose history, where you injected, and how many doses are left in each vial.

  • History — every dose grouped by day, with site and time
  • Body — anatomical heatmap showing which sites are cooling down vs. overused
  • Inventory — vial-by-vial countdown to "use by" (28-day shelf life), with vendor + lot info
  • Scan a vial label to add it; we'll pull the vendor grade automatically
04
Coach

AI that knows your stack.

Powered by Claude. Ask anything about your peptides — timing, side effects, stacking, sourcing, cycling. It already knows what you're running, what you've logged, and what's in your fridge, so the answers are about your protocol, not generic advice.

  • Trained on the latest peptide research
  • Reads your stack + recent doses + vial inventory automatically
  • Stays in scope — won't pretend to be your doctor
  • Free to try. Upgrade if you want unlimited messages
05
Library

50+ peptides. Curated stacks.

Browse every major peptide with plain-English explanations. Mechanism, typical dose, half-life, recommended timing, and risks — all written for humans, not biochemists.

  • Search and filter by goal (healing, muscle, sleep, longevity, etc.)
  • Tap any peptide for its full profile
  • Curated stacks (Wolverine, Cortex, Sleep & Repair, more) load straight into Today
  • Tap a stack to see which peptides go in and how to schedule them
